CODE QUIZ

First, select a difficulty mode. The questions are the same, but you will have less time to answer them in hard mode.


Once you have selected a difficulty mode, click the "Start Quiz" button. The timer will immediately start counting down. If it reaches 0, the quiz will end.


Questions will appear one at a time. Click on your answer. You will be informed whether your answer was correct or incorrect when the next question appears. For each incorrect answer, there is a 10-second time penalty.
